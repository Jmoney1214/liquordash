# Legacy Wine & Liquor — Delivery Zones

## Local Delivery

- **Area:** Sanford, FL and surrounding neighborhoods
- **Partners:** DoorDash and Uber Eats
- **Delivery Hours:** During store operating hours
- **Minimum Order:** Contact store for current minimum
- **Estimated Time:** 30–60 minutes depending on distance and demand
- **Age Verification:** Required at delivery — valid ID checked by driver

## How to Order Delivery

1. Call the store at (407) 878-7003
2. Or order through DoorDash / Uber Eats apps (search "Legacy Wine & Liquor")

## Nationwide Shipping

- Ships to 47 states (restrictions apply to certain states)
- Free shipping on orders over $150
- Standard delivery: 2–5 business days
- Adult signature (21+) required at delivery
- Temperature-sensitive items may have seasonal shipping restrictions

## What We Cannot Deliver

- We cannot deliver to P.O. boxes
- We cannot ship to states that prohibit alcohol shipments
- We cannot deliver to visibly intoxicated individuals
