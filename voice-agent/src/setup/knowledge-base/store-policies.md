## Pricing & Discounts

- **Case Discount:** 10% off when you purchase 6 or more bottles (mix and match)
- Prices are competitive with or below major retailers
- Special pricing available for events and bulk orders — ask a team member

## Shipping

- Available to **47 states** (cannot ship to certain restricted states)
- **Free shipping** on orders over $150
- Standard shipping: 2–5 business days
- All shipments require an adult signature (21+) at delivery

## Age Verification

- You must be 21 or older to purchase alcohol
- Valid government-issued photo ID required for all deliveries and in-store purchases
- No exceptions to age verification policy

## Returns & Exchanges

- Unopened bottles in original condition may be returned within 14 days with receipt
- Damaged or defective products will be replaced
- Special orders and allocated products are non-refundable
- Contact the store for return authorization
